## Domain (challenge region)

Northeast China (Heilongjiang + Inner Mongolia edge), approx. lat 46-49°N, lon 123-127°E.

Single-season cropping: sowing Apr-May, harvest Sep-Oct. Temperate continental climate. This is one of China's primary grain belts — a strategic region for SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) monitoring.
