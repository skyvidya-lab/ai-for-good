## Why it matters for Dynamis Terra

Our [MarkovKalmanModule](../../../src/dynamis/dynamis_core.py) is a differentiable Kalman filter. The Labbe book is the canonical non-academic reference for:

- **Building intuition** for `A`, `H`, `Q`, `R`, `K`, `P`, innovation — the six quantities that define a KF.
- **Choosing Q and R** in practice (Labbe has a whole chapter on "Designing Kalman Filters").
- **Multi-state designs** (Chapter 8 — exactly our 7-phenophase state-space).
- **Non-linear extensions** (EKF, UKF) if we ever need a non-linear observation model.
- **When KF breaks** (divergence, overconfidence) — especially relevant given our v2 `trace(P) ≈ 1.07` saturation bug.
